Política de Privacidad

Effective Date: May 26, 2025

At Fixo, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information, particularly in relation to our messaging services via SMS and WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with our website or submit a request:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Location
  • Project or service details

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Send you notifications and reminders related to your project
  • Keep you updated about the contractor assigned to your project
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries

3. Messaging and Communication

By submitting your contact information through our website form (e.g. https://fixo.network/), you consent to receive communications via SMS or WhatsApp from Fixo related to project updates and contractor services.

You may receive messages such as:

  • Project reminders or status updates
  • Notifications about contractor assignments
  • Requests for feedback or additional project details
  • Support follow-ups

Message & data rates may apply.

4. Opt-In and Opt-Out Instructions

You can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying with any of the following keywords:

  • **STOP**
  • **UNSUBSCRIBE**
  • **CANCEL**
  • **QUIT**
  • **END**

To resubscribe or opt back in, simply reply with:

  • **START**
  • **YES**

Your preference will be updated automatically.

You can also reply **HELP** at any time to receive assistance.

5. Data Protection

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is used solely for the purposes stated above and handled securely.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

Email: fixo.support@cgrouppr.com

Thank you for trusting Fixo.
